Frequently Asked Questions

To register with us, please select "Register" from the home menu and complete three easy steps.

Step 1: Enter your first name, last name, phone number, address and click Next Step.

Step 2: Enter your email address, password, confirm your password and click Next Step.

Step 3: Select your preferred security questions and enter your answers. Click Create Account.

To reset your password, select "Sign In" from the home menu. Click "Forgot Your Password" to begin the password reset process.

Step 1: Enter your email address and click Next Step.

Step 2: Your security questions will appear. Answer the questions and click Next Page.

Step 3: Enter a new password and click Reset Password.

To create a service repair request, sign in to your account.

Step 1: After you sign in you will automatically be redirected to the service page. Fill out the information at the top of the page.

Step 2: You will see all of the service options. Select the checkbox of each service that was done for that customer.

Step 3: At the bottom of the page you will see 2 fields, one for Parts Amount and the other for Labor Amount. Fill out the appropriate information and click "Create Invoice". Congrats! You have successfully created a service repair request.

After you create a service repair request you will be redirected to an invoice summary.

Click the "Print Invoice" button to print the invoice.

If you are an admin, please navigate to the "User Configuration" page and follow these simple steps.

Add a New User

Step 1: Click the "Add New User" button on the top right of the user management table.

Step 2: Complete the form and click CREATE USER. The site will redirect you back to the user configuration page, and you will see the new user listed.

Edit an existing user: To edit a user, just click on the pencil icon to the right of the user and edit the information in the form.

Deactivate an existing user: To deactivate the user, click on the garbage can icon to the right of the user name. A dialogue box will appear to confirm the decision to deactivate the user.

If you are an admin, please navigate to the "Purchases By Service" page.

You will see a pie graph with all the purchases color-coded.

If you hover your mouse over a specific part of the pie graph you will see exactly how many of that service was performed.
